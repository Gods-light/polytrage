Council verdict (4 voices, 2026-07-16)

Consensus: pursue as a measurement instrument, not live trading. n=1 event is an anecdote — 20–30 events before any tuning claim.

  • Fixed: lookahead bias — fills now at entry, peak is reporting-only
  • Fixed: slippage was optimizer-searchable → walked to $0 (Goodhart); costs now frozen constants
  • Fixed: CLI and evolve used different grids → params.json vs baseline disagreed; single canonical grid now

Open risks: single-match walk-forward folds share one liquidity regime; legging risk across 3 books is unobservable in paper trading; best edges fire seconds after goals where co-located bots race cancels — “the backtest may price an opportunity that structurally belongs to someone else.”

Next milestone: WSS order-book depth capture during detected windows on live events → measure executable size at the touch → go/no-go.
